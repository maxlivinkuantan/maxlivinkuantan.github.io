About MAXLIVIN'

Make the most of your event space

MAXLIVIN' is an all-in-one event venue located in Sri Pahang Business Centre, Kuantan. The reception area is ideal for registration, discussion and guest arrival, while the training/meeting hall is equipped for meetings, courses and workshops.

We aim to make venue rental simple: equipment can be checked in advance, seating can be adjusted according to your needs, and enquiries are handled quickly through WhatsApp.
